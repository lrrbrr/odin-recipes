Misia's protein-packed banana bread

See above the mastermind behind the protein-packed banana bread, after multiple trials of the recipe during the creation process. The founder is here under the effect of a protein-overdose, in her case, manifested as an overload of energy and chatter.

Recipe description:

Feeling a sweet but healthy snack, working home and too lazy to get out of the house? Worry no more: we have just the perfect recipe for you!

Spend less than 20-min making this delicious but also healthy and filling banana bread recipe, which you can tailor to your taste using different spices but also quantities of proteins, by adding various type of protein powder. Most ingredients can be found in any normal person's fridge - or at the closest gas station!

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe bananas
  • 3 medium-sized eggs
  • 1 pack vanilla sugar
  • 3 tbs healthy coconut sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbs apple mousse
  • ground cardamom (optional)
  • protein powder (optional)

Steps:

  1. Mash the bananas with a fork
  2. Mix the eggs in, whip for a while until foamy
  3. Add all the dry ingredients
  4. Cover the mold with parchment paper, pour in the mix

Pro-tip:

The banana bread can be frozen for up to 3 months in an airtight container or freezer bag.
